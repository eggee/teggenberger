Welcome to my page

Network Test Engineer with a diverse background in Broadband and Carrier-Ethernet network equipment, deployment, provisioning, troubleshooting and test. Solid background with manual and automated test-plan development, and execution.

I'm presently working our SDX-Fiber (Software Defined GPON, XGS-PON, NGPON2) network hardware and lab deployments using open-source hardware and operating-systems (linux).

I also have recent experience with DevOps, Agile, Python Scripting, RobotFramework, Jenkins and GitHub. Atlassian Jira and Confluence. Fluent in Windows, MacOS, and several Linux distributions, Office 365 & Teams, Visio Diagramming, SSH/Telnet(terminal emulation).

Extensive network and telecomms experience with carrier wan solutions as well as layer 2 Broadband Access technologies: Gpon/XGS-Pon/Ngpon2 (fiber networking), DSL technologies, MetroEthernet, DS3/DS1/Sonet. Test lead for Broadband product portfolio to include Voip (Sip/MGCP) as well as Legacy voice systems; GR303, TR08.

Husband, father, veteran, lifelong learner, Guitar enthusiast, always on.
